PESARATTU RECIPE

Pesarattu are moong bean crepes made with whole moong lentils, ginger, cumin and salt. This crisp crepes make for a great gluten-free & protein packed breakfast. Serve it alongside upma and ginger chutney

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole green moong
  • 2 tbsp raw rice (optional)
  • 1–2 green chilies
  • 1-inch ginger
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil or ghee for cooking
  • Optional: chopped onions
  • Optional: chopped green chilies
  • Optional: chopped coriander
  • Optional: upma for serving

Instructions

  1. Rinse moong and rice thoroughly.
  2. Soak them for 4–6 hours or overnight.
  3. Drain water and transfer to a blender.
  4. Add ginger, green chilies, cumin, and salt.
  5. Blend with enough water to make a smooth, pourable batter.
  6. Heat a tawa and lightly grease it.
  7. Pour a ladle of batter and spread it in a thin circle.
  8. Optionally sprinkle onions, chilies, and coriander on top.
  9. Drizzle oil or ghee around the edges.
  10. Cook until edges turn golden and the dosa becomes crisp.
  11. Serve hot with ginger chutney or coconut chutney.
